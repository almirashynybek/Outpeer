QS ranks MIT the world’s №1 university for 2024-25.

MIT has again been named the world’s top university by the QS World University Rankings, which were announced today. This is the 13th year in a row MIT has received this distinction.MIT was also ranked the world’s top university in 11 of the subject areas ranked by QS, as announced in April of this year.MIT also placed second in five subject areas: Accounting and Finance; Architecture/Built Environment; Biological Sciences; Chemistry; and Economics and Econometrics. QS has also released a ranking of specialized master’s programs in business. MIT ranked first for its program in supply chain management and second for its program in business analytics.

MIT Subject Rankings

  1. Supply Chain Management: 1st
  2. Business Analytics: 2nd
  3. Accounting and Finance: 2nd
  4. Architecture/Built Environment: 2nd
  5. Biological Sciences: 2nd
